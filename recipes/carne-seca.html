Carne Seca

What is it?

Carne-seca ('dried meat' in spanish) is a kind of dried, salted meat, usually beef. Carne seca is a frequent accompaniment to hot sauce and onions.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds venison
  • Juice of 3 to 4 limes
  • 2 tablespoons fine-grained salt
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons dried, ground chiles or paprika
